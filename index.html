TP6: Twisty Cookies

Ingredients

  1. ½ cup white sugar
  2. 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. ½ cup butter, softened
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 2 cups all purpose flower
  6. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. ½ teaspoon salt
  8. 1 cup chopped almonds
  9. 3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  10. 2 teaspoons rum
  11. ½ teaspoon whisky

Kitchen Equipment

  1. Oven
  2. 13" x 9" pan
  3. Measuring cups
  4. Can opener, probably
  5. Knife

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees(190 degrees C).
  2. Combine white and brown sugar, butter and eggs. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt. The dough will be stiff.
  3. Stir in nuts, chocolate chips, rum and whiskey. Drop dough by tablespoonful onto cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
